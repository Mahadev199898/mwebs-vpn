How To Connect

Follow these three simple steps to activate your secure connection.

Step 1: Get Your Access Key

After a successful payment, navigate to the My Account page. Here you will find your unique `ss://` access key. Click the 'Copy Key' button to copy it to your clipboard.

Step 2: Install the Outline App

Download and install the official Outline Client for your operating system. It's free and secure.

iOS Android Windows macOS

Step 3: Add Key & Connect

Open the Outline app. It should automatically detect the access key on your clipboard and ask to add it. If not, click the '+' icon in the top right. Paste your key when prompted, click 'Add Server', and then 'Connect'. You are now secure!
