Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2025

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy describes our policies on the collection and use of your information.

Information We Collect

The only piece of personal information we collect and store is the **email address** you provide during the checkout process. We do not require any other personal information to use our service.

Information We DO NOT Collect (No-Logs Policy)

We operate under a strict **no-logs policy**. To protect your privacy, we specifically DO NOT collect, monitor, or store any of the following:

  • Your originating IP address.
  • Your Browse history or the websites you visit.
  • Any traffic destination, data content, or DNS queries.
  • The timestamps of your connections.

How We Use Your Email Address

Your email address is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To create and link your subscription to the access key(s) you purchase.
  • To allow you to look up your active keys via the "My Account" page.
  • To communicate with you regarding your subscription or critical service updates, if necessary.

We will never sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your email address to outside parties.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will provide notice of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: support@mwebs.io
