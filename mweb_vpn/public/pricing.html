Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a recurring subscription?

No. All plans are a one-time payment for the duration selected. Your key will simply expire at the end of the term. You can purchase a new plan at any time.

What cryptocurrencies do you accept?

We accept a wide range of popular cryptocurrencies, including BTC, ETH, USDT, USDC, BNB, and SOL through our secure payment partner, NOWPayments.

What is Outline?

Outline is a free and open-source software that provides a secure and reliable way to run your own VPN server. We use it to provide you with your personal access key.
